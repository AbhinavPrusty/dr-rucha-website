Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy

We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please notify us as soon as possible.

  • Cancellation More Than 24 Hours Before Appointment: You will be eligible for a full refund of the consultation fee.
  • Cancellation Less Than 24 Hours Before Appointment: The consultation fee is non-refundable.
  • No-Show: Failure to attend your scheduled appointment without prior notification will result in the forfeiture of the consultation fee.
  • Rescheduling: You may reschedule your appointment at no additional charge if you provide at least 24 hours' notice. Please contact us to arrange a new time slot.

Refund Process

Eligible refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days and credited back to the original payment method.
