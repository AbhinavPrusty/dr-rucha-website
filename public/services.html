Our Online Consultation Services

Dr. Rucha Chhajed offers a range of psychiatric services through secure and convenient online consultations. Here are the primary services available:

Initial Psychiatric Evaluation

A comprehensive first consultation (typically 45-60 minutes) to discuss your concerns, medical history, and current symptoms. Dr. Rucha will conduct a thorough assessment to understand your mental health needs and formulate a preliminary diagnosis and treatment plan.

Focus Areas May Include: Mood Disorders (Depression, Bipolar Disorder), Anxiety Disorders (Generalized Anxiety, Panic Disorder, Social Anxiety), Stress Management, Sleep Disorders, and more.

Book Initial Evaluation

Follow-Up Consultation

Subsequent sessions (typically 20-30 minutes) to monitor your progress, review the effectiveness of your treatment plan, manage medications, and provide ongoing support. The frequency and duration of follow-up sessions will be determined based on your individual needs.

Focus Areas: Medication Management, Therapy Progress, Adjustment of Treatment Plans, Relapse Prevention.

Book Follow-Up

Medication Management

For patients who require or are currently on psychiatric medications, Dr. Rucha provides expert guidance on medication selection, dosage adjustments, potential side effects, and long-term medication management.

Note: Medication will only be prescribed after a thorough evaluation and when deemed clinically appropriate.

Book Medication Management Session
