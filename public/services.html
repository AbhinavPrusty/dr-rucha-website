Our Online Consultation Services

Dr. Rucha Chhajed offers a range of psychiatric services through secure and convenient online consultations. Here are the primary services available:

Initial Psychiatric Evaluation

A comprehensive first consultation (typically 45-60 minutes) to discuss your concerns, medical history, and current symptoms. Dr. Rucha will conduct a thorough assessment to understand your mental health needs and formulate a preliminary diagnosis and treatment plan.

Focus Areas May Include: Mood Disorders (Depression, Bipolar Disorder), Anxiety Disorders (Generalized Anxiety, Panic Disorder, Social Anxiety), Stress Management, Sleep Disorders, and more.
